Blue Carbon MRV System

National Centre for Coastal Research

Official blockchain-powered Measurement, Reporting & Verification platform for blue carbon ecosystems under the Ministry of Earth Sciences. Supporting India's commitment to net-zero emissions through coastal ecosystem restoration.

System Features

Complete blue carbon management with blockchain transparency

MRV Registry

On-chain measurement, reporting and verification of blue carbon ecosystem restoration projects.

Carbon Credits

ERC-1155 carbon credit tokens with transparent minting, transfer, and retirement tracking.

Multi-Stakeholder

Separate portals for NGOs, industries, and administrators with role-based access control.

Satellite Monitoring

Real-time ecosystem monitoring using satellite imagery and NDVI vegetation analysis.

AI Verification

Machine learning powered verification of project data and automated compliance checking.

API Access

RESTful APIs for integration with external systems and third-party applications.
